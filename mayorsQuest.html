Mayor's Quest

Help The Mayor collect as much crack as he needs before driving home on the 401.

Controls

Arrow keys: move
R: restart
S: toggle sound

Dangers

STREETCARS will crush you underneath them if you are not careful. However, they can be pushed from side to side.
Avoid CAMERAPHONES - they will hunt you down and destroy you.
CYCLISTS pose a similar threat - but, should they be crushed by a streetcar, their crack is yours.
Beware the PRESS - though harmless, they can block the path of your car, trapping you in an urban hellscape.
